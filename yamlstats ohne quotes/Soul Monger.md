    desc: The soul monger emits weariness in a 60-foot cube. Each creature in that area must make a 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 45 (10d8) psychic damage and suffers 1 level of exhaustion. On a successful save, it takes 22 (5d8) psychic damage.
